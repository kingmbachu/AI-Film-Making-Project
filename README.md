# AI-Film-Making-Project
AI-generated short film exploring identity, pressure, and self-worth through the story of a young Black woman overwhelmed by society’s expectations. Built using a multi-stage AI filmmaking pipeline combining visual generation, voice synthesis, storytelling, and human-led creative direction.


Still Becoming is an AI-assisted short film created during a 24-hour hackathon. The story follows a young Black woman struggling under constant pressure to “become more,” before confronting fragmented versions of herself and learning to simply exist without external validation.

The project used a multi-stage AI filmmaking pipeline involving character generation, scene animation, AI voice synthesis, sound design, and editing, while keeping storytelling and emotional direction human-led. The film explores themes of identity, ambition, burnout, and societal expectations through experimental visuals and narrative design.
