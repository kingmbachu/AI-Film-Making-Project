# AI-Film-Making-Project

AI-generated short film exploring identity, pressure, and self-worth through the story of a young Black woman overwhelmed by society's expectations. Built using a multi-stage AI filmmaking pipeline combining visual generation, voice synthesis, storytelling, and human-led creative direction.

## 📖 About the Project

Still Becoming is an AI-assisted short film created during a 24-hour hackathon. The story follows a young Black woman struggling under constant pressure to "become more," before confronting fragmented perceptions of her identity.

The project used a multi-stage AI filmmaking pipeline involving character generation, scene animation, AI voice synthesis, sound design, and editing, while keeping storytelling and emotional direction at the forefront.
